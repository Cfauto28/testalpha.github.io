FAQ

A list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) has been provided below for new or confused players.

Q: What is 16.05?

A: 16.05 is an official total conversion modification of Minecraft Alpha 1.0_16.02. It is where ideas that don't get implemented into the main branch end up.

Q: What is the point of 16.05?

A: 16.05 relies heavily on online play. This is not something the creators of the original branch want, so the code to experiment with this was branched off to our team.

Q: What platforms is 16.05 available on?

A: Currently, we support Windows Vista and above. Linux support is in development but there are no official downloads for Apple or Linux systems yet.

Q: What is the Hub?

A: The Hub is an area that allows you to see other players. You can enter into other players' worlds through this. Do make sure to behave yourselves and not have too much fun with their worlds. As for how to access this? You will have to find out yourself...

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: Report any issues here at our official bug tracker.

Q: When will the next update be released?

A: When it is ready.
